About me

I'm a final year PhD candidate in computer science at Brown University. I work in the Brown HCI Lab, advised by Prof. Jeff Huang. I research how we build and maintain social support networks over social media and other online communication. Previously, I worked as a software engineer at constructor.io. I graduated from Brandeis University in 2018, where I majored in in Computer Science and Psychology.

Research Projects

Chime
a social support system and sandbox space. Provides anonymous, emoji-only, social mood tracking for safe evaluation of socio-emotional impacts of online communication spaces. Evaluated in 90-person user study (CSCW 2024). Website
Sochiatrist
a social data extractor used to predict signals of mental health from social messaging data while preserving anonimity. Active since 2019, has extracted data from over 350 individuals from patient populations with clinical collaborators. Website
